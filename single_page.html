under the impression that

STRONG

I am of the opinion that 

buying bottled water 

is a waste of money.

Some people are 

under the impression that 

bottled water is safer, 

but that’s just not true.

I’ve heard that 

a lot of it actually comes from 

municipal supplies 

and is sold to us 

at 1000 times the price.

I believe that 

bottlers are not required 

to list the water source 

on their labels.

And 

as agencies consider bottled water to be a low-risk product, 

plants may not be inspected 

every year.

But 

I’m sure that 

tap water must be checked daily 

under a rigorous regime.
